F.A.Q.

Are the data that is presented in the website reliable?

All the data and prices that are presented in the website is reliable and factual as it is gathered analyzed from the Department of Energy itself as seen from this link: https://www.doe.gov.ph/retail-pump-prices-metro-manila

How do I use the service properly?

The app makes it easy for you to check gas prices in different gas station in NCR. On the home screen, you can see a list of gas stations and their prices for each city.

Are the gas prices updated regularly?

We rely on the Department of Energy for the latest gas prices and data. They usually update their information every week, and we reflect their changes on our site as soon as possible.

Is GasLeak available nationwide?

As of the moment, GasLeak is only available in the National Capital Region (NCR)
